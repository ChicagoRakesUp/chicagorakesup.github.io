What consists of Yard Waste

Yard waste is vegetative waste resulting from the care and maintenance of landscaped areas, lawns, and gardens.

  • Leaves

  • Grass Clippings

  • Shrubbery Clippings

  • Branches/Twigs

  • Dirt

  • Rocks/Gravel

Why do I need a seperate bin for Yard Waste

When yard waste decomposes in an Oxygen deprived environment like landfills, it generates Methane gas and Acidic leachate. Methane is a greenhouse gas, twenty times more potent than Carbon Dioxide! If the production of Methane is not controlled, it can seep underground and into nearby buildings, where it has the potential to explode. Landfills account for 18% of the total Methane production in the US. Yard waste takes up about 15%-20% of landfill space.
